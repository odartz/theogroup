About us

Africa is heralded as the next frontier of the world market today. With a growing population and an exploding middle-class, it is a continent teeming with potential.

In line with this, we believe that truly brilliant African opportunities with high potentials of growth deserve to be invested in.

The Ofamfa Group was established to harness this African potential. It was created in 2011 as an investment holding firm with keen interests in investment banking, private equity and venture capital.

We focus on impactful and highly profitable opportunities in strategically selected sectors within the African economy. These sectors include: Agribusiness, Infrastructure, Real Estate, Consumer Market (financial services, education, healthcare, FMCG etc.) and Technology.

The Ofamfa Group is based in Accra, Ghana, the heart of the emerging markets in Africa. From here we aim to create opportunities to enable for more businesses realize their potential. Our flagship project, KOLLABBO, (kollabbo.com) makes this a possibility to countless businesses across the continent.

Ofamfa is a traditional Ghanaian adinkra symbol that symbolizes critical examination, a value at the core of all our processes.
