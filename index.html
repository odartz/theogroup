ABOUT US

Ofamfa is our philosophy. It epitomizes our business culture of critically examining the ordinary to deliver extraordinary results.

As Africans, we understand the dire need for socio-economic development, and to foster this, The Ofamfa Group was established as an investment holding firm with keen interests in investment banking, private equity and venture capital.

As a business, we aim to help build a better Africa and ultimately, a better world for the generations after us. Through our subsidiaries,we are creating more efficient ways for businesses to become legacies that champion economic and social development.
