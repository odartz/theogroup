About us

“Each generation must discover its mission, fulfill it or betray it, in relative opacity."

(Frantz Fanon, The Wretched of the Earth)

The OFAMFA Group is an investment firm with interests in Private Equity and Business Incubation. We focus on Project Development, Advisory Services and Asset Management within the Agriculture, Technology, Infrastructure, Oil & Gas and Consumer Market sectors of African economies.

We invest in strategically selected socially relevant areas of opportunity with the aim of helping create an enabling knowledge society that equips and empowers individuals / organizations to improve themselves.

Our mission is to create, nurture, invest and partner strategic businesses that accelerate the development agenda of Africa (especially West Africa).

Ofamfa is an Adinkra symbol also known as “Hwe Mu Dua"; "Measuring Stick" in Akan. It symbolizes critical examination, our call to action.

Based in Accra, Ghana, The Ofamfa Groups’s investment vehicles are strategically structured to reflect the flexibility it adopts in executing it's mandate.

Ofamfa Capital Partners: An Advisory arm focused on providing strategic advisory services specific to the infrastructure, agriculture, technology and consumer market sectors.

Ofamfa Lab: A project and business incubation arm, focused on providing accelerator and incubation services to organizations and individuals helping them grow ventures from concept to commercial viability.

Ofamfa Holdings Limited: An asset management arm that forms partnerships with select asset managers. Its objective is to provide tailored opportunities to investors seeking rare and inaccessible projects in our strategic industries across Africa.
